Miba Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

Miba ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy describes how we collect, use, and handle your information.

1. Information We Collect

Contacts (optional)

If you choose to import your contacts, we access your device contacts (names, emails, and phone numbers) so you can see which of your contacts are on Miba. We store only email and phone values on our servers to match when your contacts sign up. We do not share, sell, or use your contact data for any other purpose. You can decline this permission and use the app without importing contacts.

Account and profile data

When you sign up, we store your name, email, profile photo, and authentication details. This is used to provide the app and identify you to your friends.

Location (optional)

If you use Mipo visible mode, we use your location to notify you when you and a mutually selected friend are nearby (within ~100 meters). Location is only used for this feature and is not shared with others except to enable proximity detection.

Push notifications

We store a push notification token to send you alerts about events, messages, and proximity matches. You can disable notifications in your device settings.

Usage and crash data

We use Firebase Crashlytics to collect crash reports and basic usage data to improve app stability. This may include device type, app version, and error details.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and improve the app (events, circles, Mipo, messaging)
  • To show you which of your contacts are on Miba (only if you imported contacts)
  • To send push notifications you have opted into
  • To fix bugs and improve performance

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only as needed to operate the app (e.g., cloud hosting, push delivery). Your profile and activity data are visible to friends you connect with in the app.

4. Data Storage and Security

Data is stored on Supabase (cloud database) and follows industry-standard security practices. Contact import data is stored securely and used only for the purposes described above.

5. Your Rights

You can delete your account and associated data from the app. You can revoke contact or location permissions in your device settings at any time. For questions or requests, contact us at the email below.
